<h3>This game's purpose in life is to predict the winning 
category of MTN Business App of the year award<h3>

so here are the rules
<p>1. Each number represent a category from one to 0 to 12(dice1 6+ dice2 6). </p>
<p>2. Blank dice is = 0 which means it has tossed off the stage.</p>
<p>3. Roll the dice once, The category appear on the screen is the Bozza category.</p>
